About Me

Originally from wild and wonderful West Virginia, I have made Charlotte, North Carolina my home since 2001. As a double English and theatre major at Queens University I studied great works of literature and ran around backstage designing sound and lights for various productions. Once out in the "real world" I continued to hang out in libraries like it was my job (because it was) and continued to create imaginary worlds in the theatre.

Currently I am devoting my brain to developing my next super power: coding. I am committed to life-long learning and enjoy problem solving. Coding provides plenty of exercise in both of those departments. I look forward to working with a team on the next challenge.
